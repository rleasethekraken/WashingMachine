        Changes the spinning speed in revolutions per minute (RPM).

        • Rinse Hold : The final rinse process is suspended so that the laundry remains in the water. To unload the laundry, run a drain or spin process.

        • No Spin : The drum does not rotate after the final drain process.

        • Only : To run the Spin Only cycle, press Spin for 3 seconds. When the cycle time and spinning speed appear, press Spin repeatedly until a desired spinning speed is selected. Then, press Start/Pause to start the cycle. The spinning time depends on the selected cycle.
